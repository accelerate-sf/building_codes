110.2 Approval
[OSHPD 1, 1R, 2, 3, 4 & 5] Equipment shall be approvable if the equipment meets the following requirements:

Equipment is approved, listed, labeled or certified for its use by a Nationally Recognized Testing Laboratory (NRTL) as recognized by the U.S. department of Labor, Occupational Safety and Health Administration.

When field evaluated, equipment is evaluated and labeled by a Field Evaluation Body (FEB) that is accredited by International Accreditation Services (IAS) in accordance with NFPA 790.

Equipment has special seismic certifications when required by Sections 1705.13.3 and 1705A.13.3 of California Building Code.
